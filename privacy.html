Quick Summary

This page explains what data I collect on this personal site, how I use it, and your choices. If anything is unclear, please email me at ishrakfarhan.b@gmail.com.

Not legal advice. This policy is tailored for a personal website with visitors in Bangladesh and abroad. It summarizes the local legal landscape and my practices in simple terms.

Legal framework (Bangladesh)

Constitutional privacy (Article 43): protects the privacy of correspondence and the home, subject to reasonable restrictions (state security, public order, morality, health).

Cyber law: current cyber legislation addresses cyber-offences and enforcement powers (including search/seizure in specified cases). I run this site with a “data-minimization” approach: collect the least data necessary and disclose only when legally required.

Sectoral laws: the Information and Communication Technology Act, 2006 and the Bangladesh Telecommunication Act, 2001 contain provisions relevant to confidentiality, interception, and telecommunications privacy.

Data protection (developing): Bangladesh is working toward a cross-sectoral personal data protection framework. Until then, this policy sets out my commitments and the controls I offer to visitors.

Information We Collect

  • Information you provide: contact details you share via email or forms (e.g., name, email), and any content you voluntarily submit.
  • Information collected automatically: basic log data (IP address, browser type, pages viewed, timestamps, referring URLs), approximate location (derived from IP), and essential cookies (see below).

How We Use Information

  • Operate, maintain, and improve this website.
  • Respond to messages and manage interactions you initiate.
  • Detect, prevent, and address security or abuse issues.
  • Comply with applicable laws and lawful requests.

Cookies & Similar Technologies

I may use essential cookies to run the site (e.g., basic preferences). If I later add analytics or other optional cookies, I will update this policy and, where required, request consent. You can control cookies via your browser settings.

Sharing & Disclosure

  • Service providers (if engaged): hosting or email delivery, under confidentiality obligations.
  • Legal: to comply with valid legal processes or requests, or to protect rights, property, or safety.

Never sold. I do not sell your personal information.

Data Retention

I keep personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes described above or to meet legal/accounting requirements. If you ask me to delete certain information and I have no legal reason to retain it, I will do so.

Security

I use reasonable technical and organizational measures appropriate for a personal website (e.g., HTTPS, minimal data collection). No method is 100% secure; if you have concerns, please contact me.

International Transfers

This site may be hosted or processed outside Bangladesh. By using the site, you understand your data may be transferred to other countries. Where possible, I choose reputable providers and apply safeguards that are reasonable for a personal site.

Your Rights & Choices

While a comprehensive, cross-sectoral data protection law is developing in Bangladesh, I voluntarily offer the following controls:

  • Access/Correction/Deletion: email me to access, correct, or delete personal data I hold about you.
  • Consent withdrawal: if I ever rely on your consent (e.g., optional analytics), you can withdraw it at any time.
  • Opt-out: disable non-essential cookies in your browser; I will honor browser/device settings where feasible.

International visitors: If you live in a jurisdiction with specific legal rights (e.g., EU/UK), contact me and I will apply the relevant standards to the extent they apply to this site.

Children’s Privacy

This site is not directed to children under 13. I do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Do Not Track

There is no industry standard for DNT signals, so this site does not currently respond to them. If this changes, I will update this policy.
